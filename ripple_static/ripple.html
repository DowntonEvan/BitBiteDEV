What is ripple?


Ripple is the 3rd largest cryptocurrency in the world. It is a real-time gross settlement system, currency exchange network by Ripple. Also called the Ripple Transaction Protocol, it is built upon a distributed open source Internet protocol, consensus ledger and native cryptocurrency called XRP.


What are the Pros and Cons of Ripple?

Pros

  • A medium for converting any currency instantly into any other one, without relying on a central exchange.

  • Processes transactions quickly (3.5 seconds)

  • Total Supply of 100 Billion. (only 38% is circulating)

Cons

  • There are no retailers who directly accept XRP

  • Centralized (available for use by banks)

  • Lack of acceptance
