What is a cryptocurrency wallet?

  • A cryptocurrency wallet is an application that stores the keys that enable the transactions for cryptocurrencies. These wallets are digital, but there have also been hardware wallets.

  • Cryptocurrency wallets store the user's public and private encryption keys, enabling them to interact with the blockchain. For example, when you are sending someone else a bitcoin, you are essentially signing off your ownership on that Bitcoin.
  • The keys that are stored on your private and public keys must match with your wallet's unique address, otherwise you will not be able to send or receive cryptocurrency.
  • When cryptocurrency is sent from wallet-to-wallet, the transaction is recorded on the blockchain and an increase/decrease in the wallets
